1. What Flapp is

Flapp uses computer-vision pose estimation to measure joint angles and give an automated technique score, findings, and AI-written coaching notes for side-view running and cycling clips or photos. An optional Expert Review adds a manual assessment written by a human reviewer. Flapp is offered with a free tier and optional paid plans.

Not medical or professional advice. Flapp's scores and suggestions are automated estimates and can be wrong — the pose skeleton may be misaligned and metrics may be inaccurate, especially with imperfect footage. Nothing here (including an Expert Review) is medical, physiotherapy, or diagnostic advice. Do not rely on it to diagnose, treat, or prevent injury. If you have pain or a medical concern, consult a qualified professional.
