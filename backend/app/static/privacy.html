Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 July 2026

Short version: Your video or photo is uploaded to our server, analyzed, and never sent to any third party. The clip itself is stored only temporarily on the processing server and is discarded automatically. We don't create accounts, run trackers, or sell data.

Flapp ("we", "us") is a personal project that analyzes side-view running and cycling technique from a short video or a single photo. This page explains what we do — and don't do — with your data. It is written to describe how the service actually behaves.

What you send us

  • The video or photo you upload for analysis.
  • The sport and position you select (e.g. cycling, TT/Tri).
  • Your IP address, which reaches our server automatically with any web request.

We do not ask for your name, email, or any account. There is no sign-up.

What we do with your media

  • Your clip or photo is processed on our server to detect body pose and measure joint angles.
  • For video, we optionally render an annotated "overlay" copy with the skeleton drawn on it, so you can review and download it.
  • The uploaded file and any overlay are written to temporary storage on the processing server and are not stored in a database or long-term storage. They are discarded automatically when the server instance restarts or redeploys (which happens frequently), and results are not persisted after you leave the page. We do not keep a copy for our own use.
  • Your video or photo is never uploaded to any third-party AI service. Only anonymous numerical measurements (joint angles, scores, detected issues) are sent to Google's Gemini API to generate the written coaching notes — no images, and nothing that identifies you.
Because processing storage is temporary, Flapp cannot retrieve a past analysis for you. If you want to keep your result or overlay video, download it before leaving the page.

Your IP address

We use your IP address for one purpose only: to enforce the free-tier limit (a small number of analyses per day). IP addresses are kept in the server's memory on a rolling 24-hour window and are cleared automatically. They are not stored in a database, linked to your media, or shared.

Cookies and tracking

Flapp sets no cookies and runs no analytics or advertising trackers. Your remaining daily quota is stored in your own browser's local storage, on your device — not on our server.

Third parties

  • Hosting: the app runs on Railway, which processes requests on our behalf.
  • Google Gemini API: receives only anonymous numeric metrics to write coaching text, as described above.

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with anyone else.

Children

Flapp is not intended for children under 16. Please don't upload footage of a minor without the consent of a parent or guardian.

Changes

This is an evolving beta product, and this policy may change as the service grows. We'll update the "last updated" date above when it does.
